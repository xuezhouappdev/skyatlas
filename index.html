About SkyAtlas

SkyAtlas International Inc. is dedicated to provide experiential vacation packages for clients all around the world. Led by travel planners with over 20 years of experience, SkyAtlas boasts one of a kind immersive travel experience for quality-seekers. SkyAtlas currently has a rich portfolio of featured itineraries for major tourist destinations in the U.S., especially on the east coast, along with summer camp options for teenagers.

SkyAtlas Values

SkyAtlas Travel Packages feature in-depth local itineraries with various themes. Our professional guides will take you to explore the hidden gems that only locals know – no matter you are a music lover, adventure seeker or gourmet.

  •   Locality – Travel like a local. Live like a local. Eat like a local.
  •   Immersion - Experience over sightseeing.
  •   Excellence – Best customer service at any time.
